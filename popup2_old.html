BG-151 PEANUT CLUSTERS

Makes 1 great gross (1,728) clusters

Ingredients:

55.6 cups U.S. Government Sugar
48 sticks (192 TBSP) U.S. Government Butter or Margarine
1 gallon U.S. Government Evaporated Milk
24 tsp U.S. Government Vanilla Extract
3 gallons U.S. Government Old-Fashioned Oats
1.5 gallons Spanish Government Peanuts

Instructions:

1) Preheat Raytheon Industrial Furnace to 375 degrees
2) Mix butter, evaporated milk, vanilla extract, sugar and peanuts in FR-8801B-01 Tactical Mixer
3) Slowly stir in Old-Fashioned Oats
4) Bake for 45 minutes or until golden brown
5) Deploy
